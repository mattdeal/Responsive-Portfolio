About Me

My name is Matt. I enjoy long walks on the beach and dark beer. I play 2 or 3 games of soccer a week.

I'm living the American Dream. I've got a wife, a house, 3 kids and car payments. The only thing missing is a white picket fence.

I work for UNC Charlotte as the system administrator for the Continuing Education registration system. In a previous life, I developed Windows apps for the Lincoln County department of Social Services.
